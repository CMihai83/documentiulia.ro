## Executive Summary

DocumentIulia.ro is positioned to capture significant market share in Romania's rapidly evolving digital compliance landscape. With mandatory B2B/B2C e-invoicing (e-Factura) and SAF-T D406 requirements driving urgent software adoption, the timing for launch is optimal.

**Key Market Drivers:**
- Romania has the **largest VAT gap in the EU** - driving government digitization
- **Mandatory e-Factura** for all B2B/B2C transactions (Jan 2025)
- **SAF-T D406** monthly reporting mandatory for all businesses (2025)
- Penalties: 5,000-10,000 RON (large), 2,500-5,000 RON (medium), 1,000-2,500 RON (small)
- **15% of transaction value** penalty for non-compliance
