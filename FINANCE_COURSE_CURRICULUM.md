# Finance for Non-Financial Managers - Complete Curriculum

**Course Title**: Finance for Non-Financial Managers
**Target Audience**: Business owners, entrepreneurs, managers without finance background
**Duration**: 10 hours (40 lessons × 15 minutes average)
**Level**: Beginner to Intermediate
**Language**: Romanian
**Certification**: Yes (upon 80%+ completion + final exam)

---

## Course Overview

This comprehensive finance course is designed specifically for Romanian business owners and managers who need to understand financial concepts without a formal finance background. The course uses practical examples from Romanian businesses and references local tax laws and regulations.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:
1. Read and interpret financial statements (balance sheet, P&L, cash flow)
2. Create and manage budgets effectively
3. Analyze financial ratios and KPIs
4. Make informed investment decisions
5. Understand Romanian tax obligations and planning strategies
6. Manage cash flow and working capital
7. Use financial data for strategic decision-making
8. Apply financial concepts to their own businesses

---
