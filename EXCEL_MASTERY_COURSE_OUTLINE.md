## Complete Course Outline & Structure

**Course Key**: `excel_mastery`
**Category**: Excel
**Difficulty**: Beginner to Intermediate

## Course Overview

This comprehensive Excel course transforms complete beginners into confident Excel users who can create professional business dashboards, financial models, and automated reports. The course focuses on **practical, real-world business scenarios** used daily by Romanian businesses.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:
- ✅ Navigate Excel confidently and use essential formulas
- ✅ Build automated financial dashboards for business metrics
- ✅ Create profit & loss statements, cash flow projections
- ✅ Analyze sales data and customer trends
- ✅ Automate repetitive tasks with basic macros
- ✅ Present data professionally with charts and conditional formatting

### Prerequisites

- Basic computer skills (opening files, using keyboard/mouse)
- No prior Excel experience required
- Microsoft Excel 2016 or newer (Office 365 recommended)
